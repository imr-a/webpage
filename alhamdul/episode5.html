Episode 5: Thirukkural Trials

Summary: Aishu enters the Shadow Realm for trials based on Thirukkural couplets, allying with Karan to solve puzzles and fight illusions.

Theme: Righteousness and virtue. Proverb: அறன்கடை நின்றார் நீர்மை (Stand by righteousness).

Script Excerpt: Mayil riddles: 'அறன்கடை நின்றார் நீர்மை – Stand by righteousness, or fall!' Aishu and Karan share knowledge to escape Vetri's ambush.

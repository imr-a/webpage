Episode 1: The Awakening Lamp

Summary: In bustling Chennai streets, Aishu discovers an ancient thiruvilakku that awakens her powers. She battles initial shadows using spice storms.

Theme: Awakening destiny and cultural heritage. Proverb: அறிவு ஆழம் அறியாது (Knowledge knows no depth).

Script Excerpt: Aishu flips idlis, but a wind reveals the lamp. Grandpa Muthu warns: "That's no ordinary vilakku!" She activates it, transforming into saree armor and unleashing a chili whirlwind. "What... what's happening? என் உடல் தீ போல் எரிகிறது! (My body burns like fire!)"

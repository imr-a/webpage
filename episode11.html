Episode 11: Shadow Eclipse

Summary: The team infiltrates Kala's realm during an eclipse, battling amplified shadows. Vetri redeems by aiding Aishu, drawing from Tamil eclipse lore and astronomy for intense, darkened fights.

Theme: Emergence from despair. Proverb: இருளில் ஒளி தோன்றும் (Light emerges from darkness).

Script Excerpt: Eclipse darkens the sky; shadows surge. Vetri turns: 'I was lost, but light calls me back!' Aishu fuses powers for an Eclipse Burst, weakening Kala's hold. 'இருளில் ஒளி தோன்றும் – we rise from the void!' Team escapes with a key revelation.
